About Us

The Avenza Maps platform is an all-encompassing solution for the use, distribution and sale of digital versions of paper maps to mobile devices.

Think of it as iTunes or iBooks for maps. We look to partner with map publishers who bring excellent design, diversity and value-added content to the platform.

Our Mission

We proactively seek and build relationships with map publishers capable of providing quality content with competitive prices.

If you create professional maps and are interested in finding new ways to generate revenue, take advantage of the Avenza Map Store which has access to millions of iOS, Android, and Windows users. It's free and easy to become a map publisher.
